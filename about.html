History

Buting Senior High School is an independent public senior high school located in Buting, Pasig City recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd). The school provides all strands of the Academic Track as well as Technical Vocational Livelihood (TVL) track for its senior high school program. Buting Senior High school is a newly standalone building which covers the last two programs of k to 12, the grade 11 and grade 12. The school opened on june 20, 2016 with 1197 students and 35 pioneering features.
